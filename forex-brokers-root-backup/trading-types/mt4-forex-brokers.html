    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    What is MetaTrader 4 (MT4)?

    MetaTrader 4 is a popular trading platform developed by MetaQuotes Software. It's widely used by forex traders for its advanced charting capabilities, automated trading features through Expert Advisors, and user-friendly interface.

    Why is MT4 still popular in 2025?

    MT4 remains popular due to its reliability, extensive customization options, large community of users and developers, vast library of indicators and EAs, and familiarity among traders. Many brokers continue to support it alongside newer platforms.

    What are Expert Advisors (EAs)?

    Expert Advisors are automated trading programs that run on the MT4 platform. They can analyze markets, place trades, and manage positions automatically based on pre-programmed trading strategies and rules.

    Do all brokers support automated trading on MT4?

    No, some brokers restrict certain types of automated trading, especially high-frequency strategies. Always check with your broker about their EA policy before developing or purchasing automated trading systems.

    What is VPS hosting and why do I need it for MT4?

    VPS (Virtual Private Server) hosting provides a remote server that runs your MT4 platform 24/7, ensuring your EAs continue trading even when your computer is off. It's essential for serious automated traders.

    Can I use custom indicators and EAs on any MT4 broker?

    Most custom indicators and EAs work across different MT4 brokers, but some may have broker-specific features or restrictions. Always test custom tools in a demo account before using them with real money.
