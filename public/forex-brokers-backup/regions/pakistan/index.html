Frequently Asked Questions

Is forex trading legal in Pakistan?

Yes, forex trading is legal in Pakistan through SECP-regulated brokers and for trading PKR currency pairs on recognized exchanges.

What is the minimum deposit required?

Minimum deposits vary from $1 to $10 depending on the broker, making forex trading accessible to traders with different budget levels.

Which currency pairs can I trade in Pakistan?

Pakistani traders can trade major pairs like USD/PKR, EUR/PKR, GBP/PKR, and JPY/PKR on recognized exchanges.

Are there any taxes on forex trading in Pakistan?

Yes, profits from forex trading are subject to income tax and may attract other taxes depending on your trading activity and income level.

Can I use international brokers in Pakistan?

It's recommended to use SECP-regulated brokers for maximum protection, though some international brokers accept Pakistani traders.
